Preparation of Turkey Goulash

Ingredients

  • ipound lean ground turkey
  • 1 can stewed, diced tomatoes
  • 3 gloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup teaspoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 package bow tie pasta

Procedures

  1. In large skillet over medium heat, cook the turkey until browned
  2. Stir in the stewed tomatoes, garlic, tomato sauce, sugar and basil, and then
    simmer for about 20 minutes.
  3. Bring large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8to 10
    minutes or until al dante, and then drain. combine the pasta and turkey mixture; toss and serve

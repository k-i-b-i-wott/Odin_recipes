Preparation of Tamarind Margarita

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon chili-lime seasoning(Tajin)
  • 1 lime wedge
  • 2 cups ice, or as needed.
  • 2 ounces tamarind paste concentrate
  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces silver tequila
  • 1 fluid ounces agave nectar
  • 1 fluid ounce lime juice
  • 1/2 fluid ounce triple sec

Procedure

  1. Sprinkle chili-lime seasoning onto a plate. Moisten the rim of the glass with the lime wedge.
    Press the moistened rim into the seasoning. Fill glass with ice
  2. Fill a cocktail shaker half-full with ice. Add tamarind paste, tequila,agave nectar, lime juive and triple sec to the shaker.
    Seal and shake vigourasly until outside is frosted, 10 to 15 seconds. Strain mixture into the glass.
    Garnish with the lime wedge.

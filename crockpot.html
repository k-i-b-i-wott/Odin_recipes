Preparation of Crockpot Italian Chicken

Ingredients

  • i bottle Italian dressing.
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts

Procedure

  1. Stir Italian dressing and Parmesan cheese together in a bowl.
  2. Place chicken in aslow cooker, and pour dressing mixture over chicken.
    Cover and cook on low until chicken is tender and no longer pink in the center, and the juices run clear about 8 hours.
